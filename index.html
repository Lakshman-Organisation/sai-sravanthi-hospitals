About Us

Trusted Healthcare for Families in Amalapuram

Sai Sravanthi Hospitals has been a pillar of healthcare excellence in Amalapuram and the surrounding East Godavari region. We are committed to delivering compassionate, high-quality medical care with a patient-first approach.

Located on College Road, our state-of-the-art facility is equipped with cutting-edge medical technology and staffed by experienced specialists. We believe every patient deserves the best medical care, regardless of their background.
